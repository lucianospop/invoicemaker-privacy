Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Welcome to InvoiceMaker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

InvoiceMaker stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Business information (name, address, contact details)
  • Client information (name, address, email)
  • Invoices and estimates
  • Expenses and categories
  • Scanned receipts and documents
  • App settings and preferences

2. How We Store Your Data

All data entered in InvoiceMaker is stored exclusively on your device using Android's local storage. We do not upload, sync, or transmit your data to any external server or cloud service.

3. Camera and Storage Access

InvoiceMaker requests access to your camera and photo gallery solely for the purpose of scanning receipts and documents. Images are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. When you choose to share an invoice or expense report using the Share feature, you control what is shared and with whom.

5. Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. We recommend using a screen lock on your device to protect your information.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual invoices, clients, or expenses within the app
  • Uninstalling the app, which removes all locally stored data

7. Children's Privacy

InvoiceMaker is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
