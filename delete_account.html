Delete Your Account & Data

North Invoice — lucianospop.github.io/invoicemaker-privacy

North Invoice stores data locally on your device. If you enabled Cloud Backup, your data is also stored on Google Firebase under your Google account. This page explains how to request deletion of your account and all associated data.

How to Request Deletion

1Sign out in the app — Go to Settings → Account → Sign out. This immediately stops all future backups.

2Send a deletion request — Email us from the Google account you used to sign in:

📧  Email Deletion Request

Include your Google account email address in the message. We will confirm deletion within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

Data Where stored Deleted when
Cloud backup files (invoices, expenses, receipts) Google Firebase Storage Within 30 days of request
Google account sign-in record Google Firebase Auth Within 30 days of request
Local app data (invoices, expenses, etc.) Your device only Immediately when you uninstall the app
Receipt images Your device (and backup if enabled) Automatically after 6 years, or on uninstall / deletion request
⚠️ Note: Deleting your cloud data is permanent and cannot be undone. Local data on your device is only deleted when you uninstall the app — we have no access to it.

Delete Only Cloud Data (Keep the App)

If you want to delete your cloud backup data without uninstalling the app, send us an email using the button above and specify that you want to keep using the app locally. We will delete only your Firebase backup data.

Contact

For deletion requests or privacy questions: privacy@invoicemaker.app

We respond within 30 days (PIPEDA, Canada) · 45 days (CCPA, California).
